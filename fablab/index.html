Concise Safety Rules

  • Use only tools you are certified and authorized to use.
  • Wear required PPE and follow posted procedures.
  • Keep hands, materials, and loose items away from moving or hot parts.
  • Stop and ask before using unfamiliar settings, materials, or tools.
  • Report damaged tools, failed prints, material issues, and unsafe conditions immediately.
