Hands-on requirement

After passing the online test, complete a supervised print workflow. Your teacher will approve the hands-on portion from the teacher dashboard.

Student must demonstrate

  • Import or prepare an approved STL/3MF file.
  • Select the correct printer and filament/material profile.
  • Orient the model and choose supports/infill only when appropriate.
  • Check estimated time/material and submit the print using the class workflow.
  • Monitor first-layer behavior and identify when to pause, stop, or ask for help.
  • Remove the print safely and clean the printer area.

Printer families covered

  • Bambu P1S
  • Bambu A1 Mini
  • Robo E3
  • Robo E4
