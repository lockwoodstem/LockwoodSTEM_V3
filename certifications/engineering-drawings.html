Hands-on requirement

After passing the online test, submit or show a teacher-reviewed engineering drawing for a simple part or classroom CAD model.

Student must demonstrate

  • Create aligned orthographic views that clearly describe the part.
  • Use correct visible, hidden, center, dimension, and extension lines.
  • Add complete dimensions without over-dimensioning.
  • Include units, scale, title block information, material/notes when required.
  • Use section/detail views only when they improve communication.
  • Apply revision notes or updates based on teacher feedback.

Badge unlock rule

  • Online test score of 80% or higher
  • Teacher-reviewed engineering drawing
  • Readable manufacturing/design communication
  • Proper dimensioning and revision habits
