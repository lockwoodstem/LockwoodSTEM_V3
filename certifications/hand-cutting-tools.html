Hands-on requirement

After passing the online test, complete a supervised workflow using measuring/marking tools, clamps, and an approved cutting or finishing tool. Your teacher will approve the hands-on portion from the teacher dashboard.

Student must demonstrate

  • Select the correct tool for the material and task.
  • Measure and mark material clearly before cutting.
  • Secure the material with a clamp, vise, or approved fixture.
  • Cut away from the body and keep hands out of the blade path.
  • Use files, deburring tools, or sanding blocks safely to finish edges.
  • Return tools and clean the station before leaving.

Badge unlock rule

  • Online test score of 80% or higher
  • Teacher-observed hands-on completion
  • Safe independent readiness for supervised hand/cutting tool use
  • Proper cleanup and tool storage habits
