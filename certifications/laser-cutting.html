Hands-on requirement

After passing the online test, complete a supervised laser workflow. Your teacher will approve the hands-on portion from the teacher dashboard.

Student must demonstrate

  • Identify approved and prohibited laser materials before cutting.
  • Prepare a file with correct vector/raster settings, scale, and line colors.
  • Set up the laser bed, focus/origin, ventilation, and job preview as instructed.
  • Maintain active fire watch for the full job.
  • Stop the job safely if smoke, flame, flare-up, or unexpected behavior occurs.
  • Remove parts safely, clean the bed, and report issues.

Badge unlock rule

  • Online test score of 80% or higher
  • Teacher-observed hands-on completion
  • Safe independent readiness for supervised laser use
  • Proper cleanup and documentation habits
