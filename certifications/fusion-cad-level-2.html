Hands-on requirement

After passing the online test, submit or show a teacher-reviewed Fusion assembly or advanced part model.

Student must demonstrate

  • Use components intentionally instead of loose bodies when modeling an assembly.
  • Create at least one meaningful joint or motion relationship.
  • Use construction geometry, parameters, pattern, mirror, or similar advanced workflow correctly.
  • Organize browser items and timeline features so the model can be reviewed and edited.
  • Create or update a linked drawing or export needed manufacturing files correctly.
  • Prepare the model for teacher design review with clear naming, notes, and revision readiness.

Badge unlock rule

  • Online test score of 80% or higher
  • Teacher-reviewed Fusion Level 2 model/assembly
  • Evidence of components, joints, and advanced parametric control
  • Proper manufacturing/export and design review habits
