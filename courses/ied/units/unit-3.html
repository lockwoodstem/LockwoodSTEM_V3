Unit 3 Major Milestones

This unit moves from digital assembly to physical prototype to final technical documentation.

CAD

Assembly + Fit Checks

Build the full rocket CAD assembly, verify alignment, add relationships, and revise before printing.

Prototype

3D Print + Build

Export, slice, print, assemble, and test the rocket prototype using evidence from Unit 2 tolerance testing.

Document

Drawings + Presentation

Create part and assembly drawings, parts list, balloons, notes, revision table, and final presentation.
