Final Project Choices

Students choose one tolerance tester that will produce useful evidence for the next rocket assembly unit.

OptionPurposeExample Test Features
A: Peg-and-Hole Fit TesterTests peg/hole clearance and fit.Five holes with different diameters and one matching test peg.
B: Rocket Body / Coupler Fit TesterTests how a coupler, ring, or insert fits into a rocket body.Five circular openings or ring samples with different clearances.
C: Rocket Fin Slot Fit TesterTests how wide fin slots should be before the full rocket assembly unit.Five labeled slots and a matching fin/tab sample.
