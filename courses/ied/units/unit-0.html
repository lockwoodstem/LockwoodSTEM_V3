Rocket Launch Pad Challenge Sequence

Lessons 0.7–0.12 move from challenge brief to final Unit 0 portfolio. Students use approved classroom materials only; CAD and student-created 3D printed parts come later in the course.

Concept

Lessons 0.7–0.8

Students define the problem, research launch pad features, create individual sketches, combine ideas into a team concept, and turn the concept into a build plan.

Prototype

Lessons 0.9–0.10

Teams build, test, and improve a physical launch pad prototype using only approved classroom materials and the teacher-provided rocket for fit checks.

Portfolio

Lessons 0.11–0.12

Teams complete final documentation, design review evidence, and an individual reflection as the official end of Unit 0.
