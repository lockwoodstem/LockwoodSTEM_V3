Units 0-5

Each unit includes a project, final evidence, and daily lesson sequence.

Unit 0

Engineering Design Foundations

Students build the habits, safety practices, documentation routines, and problem-solving mindset used throughout the engineering course.

Project: Rocket Launch Pad Design Challenge
Final Evidence: Engineering Safety Readiness + Unit 0 Portfolio

Open Unit 0
Unit 1

Technical Sketching & Engineering Documentation

Students communicate engineering designs through sketching, measurement, tolerances, assembly documentation, BOMs, change records, and final proposal presentations.

Project: 3D Rocket Assembly Reverse Engineering Documentation Package
Final Evidence: Technical Sketching + Engineering Documentation Portfolio

Open Unit 1
Unit 2

CAD Modeling, Parametric Design & 3D Printing

Students create accurate CAD part models, prepare files for 3D printing, complete the 3D printer workflow, and print a tolerance tester that supports the next rocket assembly unit.

Project: 3D Printed Rocket Assembly Tolerance Tester
Final Evidence: Fusion CAD Level 1 + 3D Printing Certification

Open Unit 2
Unit 3

CAD Assemblies, Mechanisms & Technical Drawings

Students build a full rocket CAD assembly, check fits and motion, 3D print and build a prototype, test the assembly, and create technical drawings for final presentation.

Project: Final Rocket Assembly Prototype & Design Package
Final Evidence: Fusion CAD Level 2 + Engineering Drawings Portfolio

Open Unit 3
Unit 4

Prototyping, Testing & Iteration

Students prepare, fabricate, test, analyze, and revise prototypes using manufacturing constraints, safety expectations, engineering data, and evidence-based iteration.

Project: Aerospace Prototype Validation Challenge
Final Evidence: Fabrication Tool Certifications + Prototype Testing Portfolio

Open Unit 4
Unit 5

Aerospace Systems Design Capstone

Students complete a full aerospace engineering project using research, requirements, concept development, CAD, fabrication planning, prototyping, testing, revision, documentation, and presentation.

Project: Final Aerospace Design Challenge
Final Evidence: Portfolio Completion + Final Showcase
